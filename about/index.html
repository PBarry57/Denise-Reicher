My expertise, and what I truly enjoy, is developing, implementing, managing and supporting compensation systems and programs.

Denise holds anMBA inHuman Resource Management from Bentley University and aBA inPsychology from the University of Massachusetts in Lowell.

A member of WorldatWork and theInternational Association for Human Resource Information Management (IHRIM), Denise has presented at local and national IHRIM events.She was a co-contributor to Compensation Management in a Knowledge-Based World (Exercise Book), 8th/9th edition, published by Prentice Hall.

Denise developed and marketedSurvey Sense, Microsoft Access-based software to manage compensation andSalary Plan Solution, software to streamline salary planning andrecently she developed a new application, Salary Source.

She has more than 25 years of experience in compensation and human resource systems, specializing indeveloping custom applications for organizations to meet their specific compensation requirements.

Denise can also manage short and long-term consulting projects to help HR departments succeed and go beyond their organization’s expectations.

Previous Clients

Denise was an independent consultant in the greater Boston area beginning in 1996. She has worked with many organizations in the Boston area and in Southwest Florida, full-time, part-time, contract, and as a 1099 consultant.
